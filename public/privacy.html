Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/6/2024

Last Updated: 20/7/2025

Welcome to Artisan Connect (“we,” “our,” or “us”).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website, mobile app, and related services (collectively, the “Platform”).

By using our Platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  1. Personal Information

    2. When you register or use our services, we may collect:

    • Full name
    • Phone number and email address
    • Gender and location
    • Artisan details (skills, years of experience, business name, etc.)
    • Profile photo or identification documents (if provided)
  2. Usage Data

    3. We automatically collect certain information about how you use our Platform, including:

    • IP address
    • Browser type and version
    • Device information
    • Pages visited, time spent, and actions taken on the app
  3. Location Information

    4. With your permission, we may access your location to help customers find nearby artisans or services.

  4. Communication Data

    5. If you contact us, we collect messages, emails, or feedback you send for support purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Connect customers with artisans and vice versa.
  • Improve our services, user experience, and app features.
  • Send you service updates, notifications, and promotional offers.
  • Ensure security, detect fraud, and protect user data.
  • Comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests.

How We Share Information

We may share your information only in these cases:

  • With customers or artisans to complete service connections.
  • With trusted partners who help us operate the platform (hosting, payment, or analytics services).
  • For legal reasons, if required by law or government authority.
  • During business transfers, if we merge or sell part of our business.

We never sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

Data Protection and Security

We take security seriously and use several measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL).
  • Secure storage on trusted cloud servers.
  • Limited employee access to personal data.
  • Regular system monitoring to prevent unauthorized access.

However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage users to keep login details confidential.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Access or review your personal information.
  • Edit or delete your account details.
  • Withdraw consent to data collection at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account permanently.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@artisanconnect.com .

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve your browsing experience.
  • Remember login details.
  • Analyze app performance and user behavior.

You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may stop working properly.

Children’s Privacy

Our Platform is not designed for children under 13 years old.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

Third-Party Links

Our Platform may contain links to other websites or services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party platforms. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • To provide services you requested.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

Once no longer needed, your data is safely deleted or anonymized.

Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy.

When changes occur, we will post the updated version here and revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to check this page regularly to stay informed.
